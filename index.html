PhageProfiler

PhageProfiler is a multi-resolution framework for analysing the continuum of mobile genetic elements, spanning chromosomes, plasmids, and viral genomes. At the genome level, DNA language models capture organisational features to distinguish major element classes and identify intermediate forms such as phage–plasmids. At the viral level, taxonomy-aware embeddings enable biologically consistent virus classification aligned with ICTV hierarchies. At the protein level, adapted protein language models infer phage protein functions and modular genome organisation, together revealing mosaic architectures beyond discrete classification.

Source code and documentation are available on GitHub: https://github.com/PhageProfiler
